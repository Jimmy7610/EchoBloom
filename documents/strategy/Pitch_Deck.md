# Slide 2 – The Problem

## SaaS companies lose users during onboarding — and don’t know why.

Teams can see:

* drop-off rates
* churn numbers
* funnel exits

But they cannot easily see:

* confusion
* hesitation
* frustration
* emotional friction
* what users actually think in the moment

Current solutions are slow, expensive, or reactive.

---

# Slide 3 – The Cost of Ignoring This

Poor onboarding causes:

* lower activation rates
* higher churn
* wasted acquisition spend
* slower growth
* product decisions based on guesswork

Even small onboarding improvements can create major revenue gains.

---

# Slide 4 – The Solution

## EchoBloom captures real-time user reactions inside the product.

Short prompts appear at the right moment:

* How easy was setup today?
* What confused you?
* Did you find value yet?

AI instantly turns responses into:

* sentiment insights
* friction themes
* recommended actions
* trend alerts
